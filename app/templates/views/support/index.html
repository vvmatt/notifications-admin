Support process

If you have a problem, first check for known issues on the GOV.UK Notify system status page. If you subscribe to updates, you’ll get alerts showing the progress of any listed issue.

During office hours

Our office hours are 9.30am to 5.30pm, Monday to Friday.

We’ll reply within 30 minutes whether you’re reporting a problem or just asking a question.

If you’re in a team that’s already using GOV.UK Notify but are having trouble sending messages, we’ll also give you hourly updates until the the problem’s fixed.

The team are also available to answer questions on the cross-government Slack channel.

Out of hours support

We offer 24 hour online support if you have a GOV.UK Notify account and we’ve made your service live. If you have trouble sending messages we’ll reply within 30 minutes and give you hourly updates until the problem’s fixed.

You can also use the emergency email address that we gave you when we made your service live.

We’ll reply by the next working day if you have a:

  • problem but it’s not stopping you from sending messages
  • question or want to give feedback
